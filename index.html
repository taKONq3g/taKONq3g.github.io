Totem Glyphs

Select the three glyphs shown on the totem right after entering the pyramid.

Room Locations

Go to the three locations shown below. At each location, shoot the darkness shard to open the room, enter the room and shoot the glyph at the back once. If all three required glyphs are activated, deafeating Rhulk will drop a guaranteed Deepsight weapon on your first run per week.

Between Opening and First Encounter

Pyramid

Right after the totem, drop down, turn around and you'll see the shard.

Give

After the room with all the symbols, you enter a blue-ish lit room. Look straight ahead

In First Encounter room

Darkness

In the first encounter on the pyramid side (left side as you enter the arena). On top of one of the roofs you'll find a statue. The door is behind said statue.

After the First Encounter

Traveler

After the first encounter. Run until you hit the room before The Caretaker (Pink/Purple lit room). You'll find the shard behind a statue and on top of the platform. Room is underneath.

Right after the Second Encounter

Worship

After killing The Caretaker, in the final stand room, look on the wall to find the shard. The door is on the opposite side.

Between Second and Third Encounter

Light

In the jumping puzzle area. Red lit open area with the white statue and lots of enemies. Look to your left and above the white statue. Door is to your right.

Stop

In the room below the second secret chest in the jumping puzzle. The shard is along the back right wall.

Between the Third and Final Encounter

Guardian

After the third encounter, run up the left side and on the outer edge. The room will be behind you in the middle area behind some statue pillar.

Kill

In the last platforming section, jump up three levels. Turn around away from the boss arena and you'll see the shard floating. The door is on the top level of the jumping area.
